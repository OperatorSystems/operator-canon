Organizations

Purpose

Organizations are the highest-level object in Operator.

Every piece of information belongs to an Organization.

This enables Operator to scale from a single individual to the world’s largest enterprises without changing the underlying architecture.

Organization Types

An Organization represents any group that executes work.

Examples include:

* Personal
* Family
* LLC
* Startup
* Nonprofit
* Government
* Military Unit
* Enterprise
* Fortune 500 Business Unit

Operator makes no distinction between them.

Only permissions, scale, and data differ.

⸻

Design Principles

Organizations own all data.

Users may belong to multiple Organizations.

Organizations may contain multiple Workspaces.

Workspaces contain Campaigns.

Campaigns contain Objectives.

Objectives contain Tasks.

All AI reasoning is Organization-aware.

Permissions are Organization scoped.

Memory is Organization scoped.

Documents are Organization scoped.

Integrations are Organization scoped.
