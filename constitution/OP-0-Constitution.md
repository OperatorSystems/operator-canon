# The Operator Constitution
*"Technology evolves. Principles remain."*
## Article I — Purpose

Operator exists to reduce the friction between human intention and successful execution. Every feature, engineering decision, product decision, and business decision shall support this purpose. Operator is designed to augment human judgment, preserve institutional knowledge, and improve operational execution across every domain of work. Clarity shall always take precedence over complexity. Execution shall always take precedence over activity.

## Article II — First Principles
The following principles govern all future development of Operator.

1. Technology evolves. Principles endure.

2. Everything is connected.

3. Knowledge compounds.

4. Trust precedes automation.

5. AI assists judgment; humans retain responsibility.

6. Reduce cognitive load.

7. Preserve continuity.

8. Build systems that improve with use.
